# GlobeHoppers v7.5.7

- Continuous camera reacquisition follows the live vessel through the final zoom phase.
- Connected-leg and timeline-pin handoffs no longer use a competing zoom hold/release target.
- Timeline callouts render in a fixed overlay so horizontal scrolling cannot clip active pins or pills.
- Timeline rail height and padding are reduced.
- Search and destination result panels are reduced to approximately 60% of the prior width.
- Automatic Hop titles normalize whitespace between destination, month, and year.
